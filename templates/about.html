About NaviDelhi

Building the digital infrastructure for a seamlessly connected National Capital Region.

Our Story

NaviDelhi was born out of a simple necessity: navigating the vast and complex transit networks of Delhi and the NCR shouldn't require three different apps. With millions of commuters traveling daily across DMRC Metro lines, DTC Buses, and EMU Local Trains, we realized the need for a unified intelligence platform.

Our proprietary routing engine calculates the most optimal paths using real-time spatial data, giving you the power to choose whether you want the fastest route, the cheapest fare, or the journey with the fewest transfers.
